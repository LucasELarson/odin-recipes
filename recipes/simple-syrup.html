Simple Syrup

Description

Simple Syrup is an easy to make common ingredient in many cocktails. In this recipe we will go over how to make a basic simple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1/2 Cup Granulated Sugar

Instructions

  1. Add the sugar and water to a small saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Let cool, then pour into a glass jar and seal tightly with a lid.
  4. Simple syrup will keep, refrigerated, for about one month.
